The King of Plants

A Android connected mobile phone game

Project Details

Client: Course Project

Timespan: 1.5 month

Team Size: Three

My Role

Graphic Designer

UI Designer

Devleoper

Overview

This is a PvP Android game featuring a cute hand-drawn user interface. The game is played on Android mobile phones by the two players. The two players need to connect with each other via their devices. Then they control their representative characters to attack each other’s character and avoid each other’s attack. Also, there some characters, bugs, would hurt the player. When the players defeat the bugs, the players could gain extra power from some special tools left by the bugs.

Interaction

The purpose of this game is to beat your enemy. The character, the plant, needs to move and attack. When moving, four directions are defined and they are combined with jumping and moving right or left.

Regards of jumping and make attach, users need to tap the screen. Different position of the screen represent different function: jump and attack. In order to make the character move, users need to utilize Gravity . They lend the photo to the left/right, and the character would move to left/right, along with the gravity. The purpose of this interaction design is to free the small screen from additional buttons, which need extra space and would reduce the space for the main playing screen.

For different actions, the entire screen are divided into eight blocks. When users want to invoke jumping and shooting “bullets,” they need to touch the corresponding block. In addition, there is a configuration view for users to change the function of blocks on their preference. This photo shows the view users would see if they want to customize the position.

Graphics

I designed three characters, sunflowers, dandelions and peas. The character on the screen are small and would change when performing different actions, such as jumping, moving, falling and shooting bullets.

After connection, users will see this view and they may choose their characters and the “circumstance” in which they are going to fight.

The background makes players feel that they are in a flower shop. When playing, some furniture is blurred, only those in the clear foreground view are available for characters to move around.
