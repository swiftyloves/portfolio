About Me

A bridge between design and engineering

I have creative minds as well as solid technical sense. I think comprehensively and pragmatically when I design. I like to engage with people and listen to their stories.

I'm a designer.

As a designer, I observe users and their contexts to understand unique needs and design innovative and pragmatic solutions. I articulate my thoughts and respect others’ opinions. I love to create new things and help people solve problems.

I'm an engineer.

I studied EE and CS in National Taiwan University; I’m equipped with strong knowledge of computer science, such as algorithms, databases, and information retrieval. My work at Yahoo enhanced my pragmatic front-end development. I love to make things by myself. In many projects I programmed and tested the code, learning that robustness is critical as well.
