Spotluck

Help students to enjoy differnt cuisines

The U-M Taiwanese students who just move into Ann Arbor need to cook by themselves. However, they have limited cook knowledge and cooking skills. The students are only capable to cook a small range of cuisine and they cook the same food repeatedly. Finally they are tired of eating the limit variety of food. They have many constraints and don’t know how to cook “new” cuisine. The problem focus is the intention to cook cuisines they did not do before. Considering their current constraint, I design to support these students to be able to try fresh and new food.

What's the Problem?

When cooking starts to become part of the students’ lives, they encounter a series of difficulties and challenges. They have following constraints:

Kitchen Newbies

Just start to cook and have limited cook knowledge and cooking skills

New Cuisines

Be tired of same food and want to try something new

Cuisines from Home

Want to have cuisines as they have in Taiwan

Health

Desire to eat healthy

Busy Schedule

Have limit time on cooking

Goal

Overcome these constraints to cook new cuisines.
