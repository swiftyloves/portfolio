Problem Statement

News e Forum is a student media outlet staffed by graduate students from the Department of Journalism at three universities, National Taiwan University, National Chengchi University, and National Taiwan Normal University. They intend to inform more people about public issues in the aftermath of the popular “Sunflower Movement” of 2013 in Taiwan. Even more, they want to host a new media for reporting the development of the following public issues.

Design strategy

My design strategy is elegant and clear. Since it’s new media, the major part must be words and photos. Any extraordinary animation and decoration are eliminated. Also, based on the logo of the organization, I set a serious of four primary color. Dark red is the theme color from the logo. Then I use mostly white and some light red to form the pages. Black block are used to make the text more clear and I set opacity to not fully hide the photos. Regards to the articles, the most important part, I use light and dark gray to decrease the contrast of words and white backgrounds. This also make the readers less fatigued in a fixed time.

Reading Page

Light gray text make the article easy to read. Enough space between the lines server the same purpose. Also, the theme dark red is still used on the top and the date color. Different selected news are separated with some distance. Here I user proximity theory. That is, users would consider objects closer to be grouped. Also, the content all in same font and color to imply their similarity: they are all articles. The left and right arrows are fixed and convenient for users to switch different articles no mater which paragraph they are reading.

Activiy Page

This is the page for reader to see a specific activity, including title, content, date, and some brief introduction. Also, the submit button is highlighted and "pop-up” to make users prone to click.

Main Page of News

I use “water-full” design to enable endless articles pop up and this also improve user reading experiences when implement. That is, users do not have to wait util all articles show up. If the website only need to show several articles for the reader, the time needed for the users to get feedback (see articles) would be shorter. Also, the content would not fully show up. Then in a limited space, users could see more articles and select what they want to know the details.
