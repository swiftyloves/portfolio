Overview

Alice in YahooLand is a commercial film for Yahoo Science and EC Search Team. When I was working in Yahoo, I was in this Team. In 2016 April, our team need to introduce our work to the colleagues. We decide to produce a film. I was the project leader and do major ideation and communication work. This video was originally played on the quarterly internal meeting. The present was so successful and encouraged by Rose Tsou, the Senior Vice President, we decide to make the film public.

Ideation

I intended to design a story to tell audience the work our team is responsible for and our accomplishment. I came out with Alice finding her way home at first. Then after discussing and brainstorming, we decided to change the rule of Alice, from a lost girl to a curious girl. The story based on the process of Alice in Wonderland was modified. Many elements are use to describe the work of our team. After organizing information, we concluded and decided to present our work as four service: personalization, keyword search, image search, and product recommendation.

Personalization: Show different results according to usersâ€™ preferences
Keyword Search: Helps users efficiently find products based on their queries and filters
Image Search: Provide similar-looking products according to the photos users provide
Product Recommendation: Recommend other products that users might like as well

Information Organization

I used sticker and paper to gather and group information. Also I thought and select appropriate materials and decided what should be put in the story.

Brain Storming

After the initial script, my two team members and I run a brain storming. We then eliminated some scenes, changed some sequence of the scenes and combined two ideas.

After discussion with my manager, we decide to produce a split view with mobile phone animation of our app usage and the original story on the right. This is used to get audience more aware of how the service we provide integrated into our products they view.

StoryBoard

Based on the story flow, I created many sketches for different scenes. They were used by graphic designer to develop the visual pictures. Also the film and animation editors need to produce the film based on the sketches. Thus, many indication and explanation were needed to well describe the story flow.

Graphic Design

We built the personalities of the major character, Alice, and other characters in this story. The desired appearances of these characters and scenes were to be professional not cute. Thus after several discussion and research we had the final visual style of this form. Characters are like poker face and easily replace some elements like dulls. I was responsible of giving graphic feedbacks and directing the style.

Film Editing

Based on the sketch and scripts, we utilized AfterEffect to produce the film.
